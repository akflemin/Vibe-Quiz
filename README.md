To share the guest quiz, send your guests this link:

maisonordre.github.io/vibe-quiz?couple=YOUR+NAMES

Replace YOUR+NAMES with your names joined by +%26+
e.g. maisonordre.github.io/vibe-quiz?couple=Emma+%26+Luca

Guests complete the quiz, get their Vibe Code,
and send it back to you. Then enter it in the app!
